Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumia

It will review to anime Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumia. Why this anime is my favorite and what is it that interests me.

Watch Another

The history of the anime and its chronology: The anime is interesting in that its episodes are not in chronological order. This has its own charm: the viewer has to follow the story himself and put the pieces of the mosaic together in his head. In the official chronology, first Haruhi creates the SOS brigade and then there is a dive into incredible adventures, but in the anime the chronology of the episodes is changed to maintain the mysterious atmosphere. This was a unique decision that added depth to the perception of the story.

Music: Music plays an important role in creating the atmosphere of the anime. From the opening theme “Bouken Desho Desho Desho?”, which energizes you with its upbeat energy, to the famous “Hare Hare Yukai” dance at the end, all of the soundtracks add to the dynamic and fun of the action. In tense and mysterious moments, the background melodies also emphasize the mystical nature of the plot and characters' actions.

Directing: The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya anime stands out for its unique approach to directing, especially due to its unusual series chronology. Director Takeyuki Natsuno (at Kyoto Animation) emphasizes a whimsical atmosphere and creates a balance between comedy, mysticism, and philosophy. The structure of the narrative in a disjointed way only reinforces the sense of strangeness of the world created around the main character, which makes the viewer think more and more about the reality of what is going on.

All the characters feel alive with their colorful personalities, constant interactions, and hidden agendas. Each of them has unique traits that help viewers easily understand their motivations and empathize with them.
